## Packed to the procedure, not the other way around.

Each component has its place. The adrenaline is protected from light. Inside the
lid you find the national anaphylaxis protocol, and on the side a QR field opens
two training films.

## Follows the protocol, stays within reach.

The kit follows the national anaphylaxis protocol. Each component is placed where
it is for a reason. The training films are short, precise and available from the
QR field on the side.
