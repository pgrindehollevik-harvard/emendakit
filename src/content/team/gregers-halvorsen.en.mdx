Gregers is an anaesthetist at Helse Førde with a background from the Norwegian air ambulance
service. He is a certified medical simulation facilitator and has led professional development
work at several Norwegian health trusts.

His starting point for EmendaKit is simple: an emergency kit is not a product, it is part of
the procedure, and must be treated accordingly.
