## Built for the dental service's own needs.

Reaction to local anaesthesia is the most common trigger of anaphylaxis in a dental clinic.
Latex allergy is the second most common. EmendaKit Dentist is designed around these two
scenarios, without being larger than it needs to be.

Compact format, the same framework as EmendaKit Hospital, and the right size for a dental clinic.
