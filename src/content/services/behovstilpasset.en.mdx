## Together, we design the kit for your department.

Every department has its own situations. We take the EmendaKit framework: the waterproof
case, the fitted insert, the light-protective cover and the QR field. Then we fill it with
what your department actually needs.

## How we work.

1. **Design conversation** — we talk with you about scenarios, procedures and deviations from plan.
2. **Packing proposal** — we sketch and propose contents, layout and labelling.
3. **Pilot** — one or a few units for clinical testing.
4. **Adjustment and delivery** — we adapt based on experience and deliver finished kits.

The design conversation is free and without obligation. Request a conversation by emailing Kjersti.
