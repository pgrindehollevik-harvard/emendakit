Kjersti is a midwife with long experience from Norwegian maternity units and a particular
interest in professional development, medical simulation and quality work. She facilitates
simulation training for healthcare professionals and has co-authored projects at Helse Førde.

EmendaKit came from clinical experiences she wanted to see handled differently: from
"where is the adrenaline?" to "here, in its place, ready."
